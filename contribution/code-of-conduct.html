What this is

This code of conduct outlines expectations in partaking in Pamaxie's project. This might be as an employee or as a volunteer contributor. These expectations should be upheld in all forms of communication public or private. People who are violating this conduct may be banned from our community or fired from working with us in the future.

Our Ideology

Pamaxie's ideology which should be upheld by all of our contributors, employees or leaders, is that we create an inclusive environment where voices are heard. This means no matter how a persons experience, age, body size, visibility or invisible disability, ethnicity, sexual characteristics of preferences, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, personal appearance, sociology-economic status, race, caste, color, religion, sexual identity or orientation as well as forms of expression online like having a fursona, or drawn character as an online persona, we treat to pledge you equally.

We and all contributors pledge to act mature and in ways that facilitate an open source environment inclusive, diverse, creative, fun and a generally just healthy community to be part of.

Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes a healthy community is:

  • Demonstrating empathy, kindness and privacy to other people
  • Being respectful of other peoples opinions, viewpoints and experience
  • Giving and accepting constructive feedback
  • Focusing on what is not just best for ourselves, but the overall community an project
  • Accepting responsibility and apologizing for any mistakes that may have affected others in the process
  • Learning from experiences made while interacting with others and helping with the project
  • Demonstrating a healthy way to lead cool, respectful discussions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

  • Insulting someone based on their online profiles
  • Insulting someone based on their appearance or other traits
  • Trolling, making fun of people or derogatory comments and personal attacks
  • Disruptive Behavior
  • Harassment of people on other Platforms (Social Media Profiles)
  • Publishing peoples private information, which may include addresses, email accounts, IP addresses or social media information without their EXPLICIT permission in said context
  • Other conducts that could be seen as an insult or generally inappropriate behavior for a certain scenario

Enforcement

Community leaders and staff are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior in our community and will take appropriate and fair action in response to any behavior taken by community members which maybe seen as inappropriate or insulting.

Community leaders and staff have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, reject commits, code, wiki edits, issues and all other forms of contributions made to our project that do not align with these codes of conducts and will communicate reasons for any moderation action taken, if required.

Community leaders and staff uphold the right and responsibility to act outside of things noted explicitly by this code of conduct to ensure a healthy community environment. This means we can ban, kick or remove any people we don't think fit our community because of their previous actions and behavior or actions and behavior on other online sites.

Enforcement and Reporting

We encourage everyone to report issues to community moderators, but do not advise any community members to get involved personally. Please also remember that anyone who is not part of our leaders or staff should not assume any action is taken and flaunt it in front of users.

Reports will be handled in accordance of what our community moderation team sees fit.
