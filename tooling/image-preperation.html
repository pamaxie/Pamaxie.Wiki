About

Pamaxie Image Preperation is a tool we use internally for testing the reliability of our scans and sorting through data we may have collected with other tools that can be found in our Tooling Repository.
This is also used to artifically increase data size and removing bias by rotating, color shifting and setting a fixed resolution for images. 
This prevents certain biases the neural network may develop if we didn't employ this tactic. 
All these tactics / tools have been tested extensively and work in improving the overal quality and making it easier to collect a basic dataset for us to train off of.
